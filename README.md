# First passage times of Ornstein-Uhlenbeck processes to moving boundaries
- This repository we simulate the classical Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process and evaluate its first passage time over a barrier numerically, and use the result from my published paper (Link: https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/journal-of-applied-probability/article/abs/boundary-crossing-problems-and-functional-transformations-for-ornsteinuhlenbeck-processes/3130B53D1870CB723686D1A88CD9DCCA) to find the new density for a moving boundary that is a transformation of the original barrier. The Arxiv version of my paper is uploaded here for convenience.
- We also simulate the estimated density of the FPT of OU hitting a line with different slopes. We currently do not know what the explicit density looks like and it is an open question in the literature, but this gives us a hint of the shape and how close it must be to the expression we have in the case of a barrier (slope=0).
